About Iron IoT

Built around visibility, research, and protection.

The growth of connected devices has expanded both opportunity and risk. Iron IoT exists to support a safer ecosystem by making IoT exposure easier to understand and by developing defensive tools that inform better decisions.

Our work is designed to support awareness, responsible research, and proactive security improvement across connected environments.

What we provide

Three core services for IoT security operations and research.

Iron IoT concentrates on three complementary capabilities that improve visibility into connected environments, study adversarial behavior, and support early detection of weaknesses.

01

IoT Search Engine

A platform for discovering and organizing information about internet-reachable IoT systems, services, and device exposure patterns. This enables research, asset visibility, and informed security analysis.

  • Surface exposed device metadata
  • Support asset visibility initiatives
  • Improve situational awareness
Try Iron IoT Search Engine
02

IoT Honeypot

Controlled decoy systems built to attract and observe malicious interaction. This helps researchers understand attacker behavior, common probing patterns, and emerging threats against connected devices.

  • Observe threat activity safely
  • Capture behavioral indicators
  • Generate intelligence for defense
Try Iron IoT Honeypot
03

IoT Vulnerability Scanner

A security assessment capability for identifying weaknesses in IoT systems so they can be addressed before they become incidents. The emphasis is on responsible discovery and actionable remediation insights.

  • Reveal common misconfigurations
  • Highlight exposed services
  • Support remediation planning
Try Iron IoT Vulnerability Scanner

Why it matters

IoT risk is growing across homes, institutions, and critical environments.

As connected devices become more common, visibility gaps and weak configurations create opportunities for abuse. Better discovery, monitoring, and testing help organizations reduce uncertainty and act sooner.

Visibility

Understand what is exposed and where the risk surface is expanding.

Threat intelligence

Learn how malicious actors probe and interact with connected systems.

Resilience

Turn findings into more secure configurations, processes, and defensive posture.

2

3
